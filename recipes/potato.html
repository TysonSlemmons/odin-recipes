Baked Potato

Description

This baked potato has a crisp, golden skin, and is light and fluffy on the inside - a perfect baked potato. Serve with a dollop of sour cream if you like. Great comfor food!

Ingredients

  • Russet Potato
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Bake the potato at 300f until potatoes are tender and golden brown
  2. Cut open potato, fluff the center, and let cool
  3. Add sour cream, green onion, cheddar cheese, and bacon
  4. Serve up!
