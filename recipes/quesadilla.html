Chicken Quesadillas

Description

This chicken quesadilla recipe is great to make for parties. Zesty chicken, cooked peppers, and melted cheese are a delightful combination.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Quesadilla
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then broil until cooked
  2. cook the vegeables with the broiled chicken
  3. Layer mixture onto the tortilla, then top with cheese and bacon
  4. Fold the tortillas and bake until cheese is melted
  5. Cut and serve
