Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Description

A cheap and easy homemade recipe for Paneras' famous broccoli cheddar soup. Prep time only takes 10 minutes, and the soup will need to cook for an additional 35 minutes. This recipe makes 8 servings!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup matchstick-cut carrots
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Melt 1 TBSP butter, add onion, and stur until tender and translucent (approx 5 mins). Set aside
  2. Whisk 1/4 cup melted butter and flour for 3-4 mins
  3. Gradually add milk while whisking constantly, stir in chicken stock and bring to a simmer
  4. Cook until thickened (15-20 mins)
  5. Add broccoli, carrots, celery, and the onion. Simmer until all vegetables are tender (10-15 mins)
  6. Stir in cheese, salt, and pepper and serve
